## 2. Description of Service

Atom2Universe is a free Android app made of many different modules, constantly evolving. At its core is an atom clicker where you start with a single atom and collect more to gradually fill your universe. The app also includes:

- Atom clicker / idle game with gacha mechanics
- Music player for local audio files
- MIDI player with synthesizer support
- Internet radio streaming
- Audio editor with recording capabilities
- Games, creative tools, and more
